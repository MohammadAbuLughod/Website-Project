Who are the top 6?

The top 6 are the biggest six clubs in English Football in terms of trophies, viewership and club worth. They consist of Manchester United, Manchester City, Chelsea FC, Arsenal, Tottenahm Hotspur, and Liverpool FC.

    7. Liverpool

    Liverpool FC was founded in 1892 following a split from Everton FC. Since then, Liverpool has grown to become one of the biggest clubs in Europe. They have a long list of legends at the club including Ian Rush, Steven Gerrard and Luis Suarez. Their stadium, Anfield, is one of the most famous venues in the world and is known to have some of the most electric atmospheres in world football.

    Manchester United

    Manchester United was formed in 1878 and competed in their first professional match in 1880. Today, the are one of the biggest brands in the world. Their most famous manager, Sir Alex Ferguson guided the club to the next level and helped it become the global name that it is today. The club plays in Old Trafford, one of the most well known football stadiums in the world.

    Arsenal

    Arsenal was founded in 1886 and is one of the most decorated clubs in Europe. The club is most famously known for their invincible season of 2003/2004 under the club's most succesful manager, Arsene Wenger. The club has a long list of legends that played at the Emirates Stadium including premier league hall of famer Thierry Henry, Cesc Fabregas and Mesut Ozil.

    Chelsea

    Chelsea was founded in 1905 and is the only club based in London to win a European Trophy. The club was not as succesful during the 20th century but grew into one of the biggest clubs in the world after a takeover in the early 2000s from Russian business tycoon, Roman Abramovich. Since then, under various managers including Jose Mourinho and Antonio Conte, the club has won 2 European trophies.

    Manchester City

    Founded in 1880, Manchester City was not always such a big club. It was more known to be a family club with humble values. However in the late 2000s, the club was bought out by Arab billionaires and has grown into a giant in world football. They have won the most trophies in the Premier League than any other club in the past ten years and are not far away from becoming the most decorated team in English football.

    Tottenham Hotspur

    Founded in 1882, the club is one of the biggest in English football. They have failed to win many trophies in recent years and are currently struggling to find form in the Premier League but they have some very big names on their side including Harry Kane and Heung Min Son.
